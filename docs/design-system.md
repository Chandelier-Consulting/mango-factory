# Mango Factory Design System

## Direction

Premium dessert-and-drinks site inspired by the cloned Komala Vilas and Chaat On Wheels projects: dark first viewport, warm food photography, sharp direct CTAs, cream content bands, and restrained dashboard UI.

## Tokens

- Background: `#fbf3df`
- Night: `#100d0a`
- Paper: `#fff8ea`
- Ink: `#17100a`
- Mango: `#f7b51b`
- Mango deep: `#d87a13`
- Leaf: `#1d6b42`
- Guava: `#de4a31`
- Radius: `8px`
- Display font: Playfair Display
- UI font: Geist
- Motion: Framer Motion reveal, hover, tap. Reduced motion respected.

## Surfaces

- `/`: customer homepage with hero, proof, signatures, ordering flow, and marketing preview.
- `/menu`: polished menu highlights.
- `/marketing`: lightweight marketing tracker for campaigns, leads, orders, and ROAS.
